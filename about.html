About us

Red Bull, founded in 1984 by Dietrich Mateschitz, is an Austrian company that created the energy drink category after Mateschitz was inspired by a functional drink from East Asia. Driven by the motto, "Red Bull Gives You Wiiings," the company markets a lifestyle associated with extreme sports, adventure, and pushing boundaries, not just the drink itself. Red Bull operates globally, selling its energy drinks in over 170 countries and maintaining a significant presence in media, sports sponsorships, and events

The company's core product is the Red Bull Energy Drink, but it also offers various sugar-free and special edition drinks. Red Bull has a strong media presence, with Red Bull Media House producing content across various platforms, including Red Bull TV, Red Bull.com, and Red Bulletin. The company is a global operation, with products sold in over 170 countries and a presence spanning the Americas, Europe, Asia, and Africa
